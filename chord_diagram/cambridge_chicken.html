This map visualizes the 500 most popular pairings of key terms in menu items containing chicken served in 505 restaurants in Cambridge, MA.

Chicken is the most common food item to appear in menus in Cambridge, occuring more 4000 times. Approximately 10 percent of all menu items, including beverages contain chicken in one form or another.
