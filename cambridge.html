This map visualizes the 58225 food items served in 505 restaurants in Cambridge, MA.

Sometimes when recalling a neighborhood, a single restaurant comes to mind. However as a group, the restaurants of a city offer one version of its collective identity. What are the different ways chicken is made? What is described as "fresh" or "special"? What do we "roast", "fry", or "bake"? How many different "vegetarian" food items can you find in Cambridge?
